How to install

1

Download the installer

Click the button above to download VKEnterprises_Setup.exe to your computer.

2

Run as Administrator

Right-click the downloaded file → Run as administrator. Click Yes on the Windows prompt.

3

Follow the installer

Click Next → Next → Install. The app will be placed in Program Files and a shortcut added to your Desktop and Start Menu.

4

Launch and log in

Open VK Enterprises from your Desktop. Enter your mobile number and the password provided by your admin.
